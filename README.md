# Gym Membership Management System

A web-based system developed to digitize and streamline gym operations for fitness institutions, replacing manual paper-based processes with an efficient digital platform.

> 🎓 Software Engineering II Project — Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, 2024

---

## 📋 Overview

The Gym Membership Management System automates day-to-day gym operations including member registration, attendance tracking, staff management, and equipment monitoring. It features role-based access for admins, staff, and members — each with dedicated functionalities tailored to their responsibilities.

---

## ✨ Features

- 👤 **Member Management** — Register, edit, and delete member records
- 💳 **Membership Plan Tracking** — Monitor active, expired, and pending memberships
- 🏋️ **Equipment Monitoring** — Add, update, and remove gym equipment
- 📅 **Attendance Tracking** — Log and view member attendance
- 💰 **Payment Management** — Track membership payments
- 📢 **Announcements** — Post updates for members and staff
- 📊 **Dashboard** — Overview of gym operations at a glance

## 👥 User Roles

- **Admin** — Full control over members, staff, equipment, and reports
- **Staff** — Manage attendance, payments, and member status
- **Member** — View membership status, announcements, and personal info
